WHO WE ARE

The premier student body fostering technical excellence at REC Gonda

OUR VISION

We envision a campus where every student has the opportunity to explore, innovate, and excel in technology. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world applications, preparing students for the challenges of tomorrow's tech landscape.

Through collaborative projects, workshops, and competitions, we create an ecosystem that nurtures technical talent and fosters innovation.

OUR GOALS

  • Establish REC Gonda as a hub for technological innovation and research
  • Provide hands-on learning experiences through technical events
  • Enhance industry-academia interaction with expert-led sessions
  • Promote entrepreneurship and emerging technologies
  • Create platforms for students to showcase their technical prowess

OUR INITIATIVES

Engaging, challenging, and rewarding technical experiences

HACKATHON

Our flagship 36-hour coding marathon where innovation meets execution. Teams compete to build solutions for real-world problems.

  • Themes: Web Dev, AI, IoT, Blockchain
  • Mentorship from industry experts
  • Cash prizes and internship opportunities

PORTFOLIO CHALLENGE

Showcase your skills with a stunning portfolio website. This competition helps you build your professional online presence.

  • Judged on design, functionality and content
  • Feedback from senior developers
  • Featured on our website for top entries

TECH TALKS

Monthly sessions with industry leaders sharing insights on cutting-edge technologies and career guidance.

  • Speakers from top tech companies
  • Q&A and networking opportunities
  • Hands-on workshops and demos

BRAND AMBASSADORS

Represent leading tech brands on campus while developing leadership and marketing skills.

  • Exclusive networking opportunities
  • Certificates and stipends
  • Organize tech events on campus

ROBOTICS EXPO

Annual showcase of cutting-edge robotics projects with competitions and interactive demonstrations.

  • Live bot battles and demonstrations
  • Workshops on Robotics & IoT
  • Prizes for innovative projects

AI CHALLENGE

A multi-stage competition testing your machine learning and data science skills.

  • Curated datasets and problem statements
  • Mentorship from AI researchers
  • Showcase your models to recruiters

JOIN OUR MOVEMENT

By joining the Technical Council at REC Gonda, you become part of an elite team shaping the future of technology on campus. Here's what awaits you:

  • LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT: Gain valuable experience organizing major tech events and managing teams
  • PROFESSIONAL NETWORK: Connect with alumni, industry professionals, and like-minded peers
  • EXCLUSIVE ACCESS: Early registration for workshops, hackathons, and other premium events
  • CAMPUS IMPACT: Play a key role in shaping REC Gonda's technical ecosystem
  • CAREER ADVANTAGE: Enhance your resume with leadership experience and technical credentials

Being part of the Technical Council means being at the forefront of technological innovation on campus. Whether you're passionate about coding, robotics, AI, or just want to be part of a dynamic team, we have opportunities for everyone.
